About me

I am a Dubai based communications professional with an MA in International Journalism from City University London. I have several years of experience in writing, editing, research and photography. My hobbies include reading, writing, listening to music, cooking vegetarian dishes, walking, photography and feeding stray cats. I've also recently started tinkering with electronics and microprocessors namely, the Arduino and the Raspberry Pi boards. I've discovered that combining hardware and software is bringing me lots of joy and encourages mindfulness. In addition to this, I am presently enrolled in a part-time web development bootcamp at AstroLabs in the United Arab Emirates. I hope to use the skills learned to pivot into web development and the burgeoning world of technology.
